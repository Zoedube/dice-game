Game Instructions

Understanding the buttons: the new game button, refreshes the game.The roll dice button rolls the dice and the hold button shows the score of the two players.

Understanding the contrasting colors: The white on the background of the players panel determines who is currently playing. Which is also indicated by the dice icon next to the players name.

To start the game first enter your name without any special charcters i.e no numbers.

To win the game you have to accumulate a hundred points.The player loses his score entirely if they roll a six two times. And if they roll a one they lose their turn.
